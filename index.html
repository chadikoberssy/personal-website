My Favourite Foods

I am Lebanese and I love our cuisine as so do most of the people. Lebanese cuisine truly is a delight to the senses. From the earthy aroma of cumin and tahini to the refreshing pop of sumac, pomegranate, and lemon, Lebanese food takes you on a journey. A journey that starts with the colors, aromas, and textures of mezze and ends with the fragrance of desserts flavored with rose water and honey.
